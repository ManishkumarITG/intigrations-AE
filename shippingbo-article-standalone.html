Shippingbo Integration — Shopify Order Editing Sync (Account Editor)

3PL Integrations  •  Last updated: April 2026
📋 Required Setup — Enable Manual Payment Capture in Shopify
✅ Step-by-Step Configuration
  1. 1 Log in to Shopify Admin
  2. 2 Go to Settings → Payments
  3. 3 Scroll down to the Payment capture section
  4. 4 Select "Manually capture payment for orders"
  5. 5 Click Save
⚠️
Important: Payment authorizations expire after 7 days (Shopify Payments). Always capture payment within this window — or the authorization will lapse and you won't receive payment for the order.
🔌 Open Connectors in Shippingbo
✅ Step-by-Step Configuration
  1. 1 Log in to your Shippingbo account
  2. 2 On the left sidebar, click Connectors

Shippingbo Dashboard — Click 'Connectors' in the left sidebar (highlighted in red)

🛍️ Open Your Shopify Connector Settings
✅ Step-by-Step Configuration
  1. 1 On the My Connectors page, find your store listed under Configured
  2. 2 Click on the Shopify block to open its settings

My Connectors — Click your Shopify store block (highlighted in red)

⚙️ Set the Exclusion Rules
✅ Step-by-Step Configuration
  1. 1 Inside connector settings, scroll down to the Orders retrieving section
  2. 2 Find "Exclude by payment status" — add these tags:

    authorized partially_paid
  3. 3 Find "Exclude by order status" — add these tags:

    on-hold on_hold
  4. 4 Save your settings

Connector Settings — Add exclusion tags in both fields as shown (highlighted in red)

📝
The value authorized corresponds to Shopify's financial_status field. Shippingbo will skip these orders entirely until payment is captured and status becomes Paid.
✏️ Edit the Order, Then Capture Payment
✅ Your New Workflow (Every Order)
  1. 1 New order arrives → Payment = Authorized (Shippingbo skips it ✅)
  2. 2 Open the order in Account Editor — add or remove items freely
  3. 3 Save / commit your edits
  4. 4 Go to Shopify Admin → open the order → click Capture Payment
  5. 5 Payment becomes Paid → Shippingbo downloads the final correct order ✅
🔄 What Happens After This Setup

Once configured, Shippingbo will always receive the final edited order:

  • ✅ Correct order total (removed items not counted)
  • ✅ Added items appear correctly
  • ✅ Removed items are excluded from total
  • ✅ Quantity changes respected
  • ✅ No manual correction needed in Shippingbo
✅
Setup complete! Shippingbo will always receive the final, correctly edited order from now on.
⚡ — Automate Payment Capture with Shopify Flow
🔄 Optional Automation to Streamline Your Workflow

Instead of manually capturing payment on every order, set up a Shopify Flow automation to manage capture while keeping a review window for normal Online Store orders.

1Trigger

A new order is created in your store.

2Condition 1 — Sales Channel Check

The flow checks whether the order came from the Online Store.

  • If NOT from Online Store (for example, POS or draft order) → capture payment immediately.
  • If from Online Store → continue to the next check.
3Condition 2 — Fraud Risk Check

Shopify’s fraud analysis checks the order risk level.

  • If risk level is HIGH → capture payment immediately (review these orders manually).
  • If risk is not HIGH → continue to the next check.
4Condition 3 — Restricted Tag Check

The flow checks whether the order is tagged restricted.

  • If tagged "restricted" → capture payment immediately.
  • If not restricted → wait 15 minutes, then capture payment.
📋 Summary
Scenario Action
Order from other channels (not Online Store) Capture immediately
Online Store + HIGH risk Capture immediately
Online Store + tagged "restricted" Capture immediately
Online Store + normal order Wait 15 min, then capture
